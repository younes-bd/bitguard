# BitGuard Enterprise Platform: IT & Cybersecurity Company Management Guide

Welcome to BitGuard! This guide will walk you through managing your IT and Cybersecurity services company using your new unified management platform.

---

## 🚀 1. Getting Started: The Command Center
The **BFF Command Center** is your daily starting point.
- **KPI Cards**: Click any card (Total Revenue, Active Tickets, etc.) to jump straight to technical details.
- **Quick Actions**: Use the grid at the bottom to jump to common tasks like "Add New Client" or "Provision Service".
- **System Health**: Monitor the status of your core ERP and Security database in real-time.

---

## 💼 2. Managing Clients (CRM)
Your business revolves around clients.
1. **Create a Client**: Navigate to **Sales & CRM > Clients**. Click **+ New Client**.
2. **Detailed Profile**: Click any client in the list to see their **Unified Profile**.
   - **Overview**: See active contracts and recent interactions.
   - **Assets**: View managed infrastructure (licenses, hardware) currently assigned to them.
   - **Timeline**: Track Every phone call, email, or meeting via the Activity Timeline.
3. **Deals**: Track your sales pipeline under **Sales & CRM > Deals**. Drag and drop (or update status) to move projects from "Discovery" to "Contract Signed".

---

## 🛠️ 3. Managing IT Services & Products (Store)
You provide both one-off services and recurring subscriptions.
1. **Product Catalog**: Go to **E-Commerce > Products**.
   - **Services**: Create a "Managed SOC" or "Network Audit" service.
   - **Inventory**: Track physical hardware (Firewalls, Laptops) if you sell them.
2. **Order Management**: When a client buys a service, it appears in **E-Commerce > Orders**.
   - Click an order to see who bought what.
   - Ensure you fulfill technical tasks once payment is verified.

---

## 🔒 4. Cybersecurity Operations (SOC)
As a Cybersecurity company, the **SOC Dashboard** is critical.
1. **Monitoring Alerts**: Go to **SOC > Alerts**.
   - **Rose Badge**: Critical threats found by BitGuard sensors.
   - **Amber Badge**: Suspicious activity requiring investigation.
2. **Incidents**: When an alert is verified, create an **Incident**. Track its resolution status here.
3. **Asset Inventory**: See every endpoint (computer/server) you are protecting for your clients.

---

## 🏗️ 5. Project Management
For complex migrations or security implementations:
1. **Create Project**: Go to **Projects > All Projects**.
2. **Assign Tasks**: Link tasks to specific employees and clients.
3. **Track SLAs**: The ERP dashboard will show you if you are meeting your Service Level Agreements (SLA) for these projects.

---

## 📊 6. Financials & Billing (ERP)
1. **Invoicing**: Go to **ERP > Invoices**.
   - The system automatically detects orders from the store.
   - You can manually create invoices for custom consulting work.
2. **Revenue Health**: Visit the **Analytics & Reports** module to see your **MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue)** trend. This is the heart of a tech company's health.

---

## ⚙️ 7. System Administration
For the owner (System Admin):
1. **Identity (IAM)**: Manage company users and their permissions under **Identity & Access**.
2. **Maintenance**: Use the **System Admin** dashboard to:
   - Clear system cache for performance.
   - Sync search indexes.
   - Toggle "Maintenance Mode" if you are performing platform upgrades.

---

## 💡 Pro-Tips for the New Founder
- **Unified Vision**: Always check the **Command Center** first thing in the morning.
- **Data Integrity**: Log all client communications in the CRM Timeline. It prevents "lost knowledge" when your team grows.
- **Automation**: Use the **Marketing** module to send automated trial follow-ups to new leads captured in the Store.

Congratulations on starting your IT & Cybersecurity venture! BitGuard is built to scale with you.
