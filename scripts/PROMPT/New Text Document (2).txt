Service Definition (apps.erp.models.Service):

You define your standard offerings here.
Action: Ensure every service has a base_price and a service_type (hourly/project/subscription).
CRM (apps.crm.models):

Lead to Opportunity: Sales rep talks to a Client.
Drafting the Deal: They create a Contract draft. At this stage, you generate the Proforma Invoice.
Technical Flow: Order -> Proforma Invoice PDF.
Result: Client confirms.
ERP / Operations (apps.erp.models):

Once the Contract is signed, an InternalProject is created.
Resource Allocation: You assert people (EmployeeProfile) to the project.
Work Logging: They log time (TimeLog), which has a cost (salary) and a billable rate (price).
Invoicing (apps.erp.models.Invoice):

This is the bridge between CRM and ERP.
Recurring: Automatically generated every month for Subscriptions.
Milestone: Generated when a Project Milestone is marked "Complete" (e.g., "Deployment Done" -> Trigger Invoice).
Financials:

Revenue = Invoice.total (excluding tax).
Cost (COGS) = TimeLog.hours * Employee.cost_rate + Expenses.
Profit = Revenue - Cost.
